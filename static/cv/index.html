An introduction to me and my career, written with hiring managers in mind.

by Derek Odegard

Since 1996, I have evolved from a self-taught dabbler in building web sites, to a full-stack developer of web apps, then to a front end specialist, and finally to a builder and manager of teams of front end developers. In all likelihood, my next gig will involve hiring and leading front end developers. I enjoy doing it, and I am told that I'm very good at it.

However, truth be told, I'm itching to try something new.
